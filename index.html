About Us

GreenEdge Landscape is a professional landscaping company proudly based in Cape Coast, Ghana. We specialize in designing, creating, and maintaining beautiful, sustainable, and functional outdoor spaces for both residential and commercial clients. Our team of skilled professionals is passionate about transforming ordinary spaces into extraordinary environments that reflect the unique vision and needs of our clients.

At GreenEdge Landscape, we believe that outdoor spaces should not only be visually appealing but also environmentally friendly. That’s why we incorporate eco-conscious practices and innovative solutions into every project we undertake. From lush lawns and vibrant gardens to efficient irrigation systems and modern hardscapes, we offer a wide range of services tailored to meet your landscaping goals.

With a commitment to excellence, attention to detail, and customer satisfaction, we strive to exceed expectations and deliver results that enhance the beauty and value of your property. Whether you’re looking to create a serene garden retreat, a functional outdoor workspace, or a welcoming commercial landscape, GreenEdge Landscape is here to bring your vision to life.
